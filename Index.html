Mentorship

No mentorship program can work on a one-size fits all model. Different students have different needs and Nudgy realizes this. Our mentorship program is aimed at addressing the personalised needs of the mentee directly. This means that the nature and course of mentorship is directed by mentee

Knowing the Mentee

In this step, the assigned mentor interacts with the student / professional and reflects on the needs, circumstances, and requirements. Consider this to be a meet and greet session.

GOAL PLANNING

Once the student / professional and the mentor are well versed with each other and the mentor has realized the needs of the student / professional, the mentor provides the best possible ways to achieve this goal. This depends on each mentor and the purported goal. For example, if the goal is to become a corporate lawyer, then the mentor may find it fit to teach the mentee the basics of drafting corporate legal texts. In short, the mentorship pathway is the combination of mentor and mentee’s efforts.

YOUR MENTOR

The right mentor(s) are chosen as per the mentee’s desired career goal. This depends on various factors such as availability, requirements, and recommendations by senior members. Our aim is to connect the right mentor with the right student / professional. With the right mentorship in place, Nudgy aims to expand the legal skills of the mentees which make them competitive in the legal industry.
